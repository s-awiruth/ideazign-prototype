Visual Hierarchy

Three core solutions dominate via size, white cards and UI previews; entry points are smaller neutral tiles. In the decision flow, human steps use filled orange while AI/System are muted — humans read as more prominent by design.

Responsive Behavior

Fluid clamp() type/spacing plus auto-fit grids reflow from the 12-col desktop layout to a single 4-col mobile stack. Cards wrap, the hero collapses to one column, and nav condenses — no fixed breakpoints to maintain.

Interaction & Motion

Restrained: card hover-lift on the three core solutions, a single hero fade-in, and smooth in-page anchor scrolling. Motion is used only where it aids understanding — no autoplay or decorative glow.

Reusable Components

Header/footer, section header block, solution card, entry-point tile, status label, mini-UI preview frame, step tile, control chip, showcase card and CTA band all carry to Solutions, Showcases, How-We-Work and Contact pages.
