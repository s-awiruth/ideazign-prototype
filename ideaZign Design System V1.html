05 · APPLICATION PRINCIPLES

Visual Hierarchy

Three core solutions dominate via size, white cards and UI previews; entry points are smaller neutral tiles. Human decision steps use filled orange while AI/System are muted — people read as most prominent by design.

Interaction & Hover

Core solution cards lift −4px with a soft shadow on hover; buttons darken to #D9500F; text links shift to orange. A single hero fade-in and smooth anchor scrolling. No autoplay or decorative glow.

Responsive Rules

Fluid clamp() + auto-fit grids reflow 1440 → 390 continuously. One 640px breakpoint condenses the header. Body/label never below 12–13px; generous 1.5–1.6 line height for mixed Thai/English.

Brand & Logo

Official logo only, original colors and proportions, clear space preserved, on white when placed on dark. Orange is a UI accent and is never applied to the logo. Max two background tones per view.
